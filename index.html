OBJECTIVE

Earn the most Kingdom Points after 7 rounds.

  1. Read the challenge.
  2. Play one parable card.
  3. Match the virtue for bonus points.
  4. Resolve the card ability.
  5. Highest score wins.
This game is designed for reflection and strategy, not for replacing Bible study.

WHAT ARE KINGDOM POINTS?

Kingdom Points (KP) are your score. The player with the most KP wins.

WHAT ARE VIRTUES?

Faith

Trust God.

Mercy

Help others.

Wisdom

Make good decisions.

Generosity

Share blessings.

Game Modes

  • Single Player: Face The Trial. Easy is loose, Medium favors matching virtues, and Hard scores aggressively.
  • Multiplayer: Pass the device. Hands stay hidden until the next player begins.
  • Table Mode: Groups discuss three parables and choose one together.

Card Abilities

  • Abilities add small strategic effects: gain KP, draw, recover, prepare next round, or help the lowest-scoring player.
  • The result panel shows exactly what happened after each card.

Providence Cards

  • After a challenge appears, Providence has a 25% chance to add a round twist.
  • If no Providence card appears, the round plays normally.

Table Mode

  • Reveal a challenge.
  • Discuss three parable cards.
  • Choose the card that best speaks to the situation.
  • Use the discussion prompts after scoring.

Multiplayer

  • Round and player position are shown each turn.
  • Use the pass screen to protect each player's hand.

Printing the Deck

  • Print parable, challenge, Providence, card backs, quick-start cards, tokens, rules, and score sheets.
  • Print mode uses white backgrounds, dark text, virtue stripes, and cut guides.

Strategy Tips

  • Match the challenge virtue when you can.
  • Sometimes a useful ability is worth more than a match.
  • Watch the lowest score. Some cards can change the table quickly.
  • Your Disciple Profile reflects your strongest virtue.
