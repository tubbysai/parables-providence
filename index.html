📜 About Parables & Providence

A Christian strategy card game where players wield the teachings of Jesus to overcome challenges and build God's Kingdom. Each card represents one of the parables of Christ, carrying a virtue, a special ability, and Kingdom Points.

🎯 Objective

Single Player: Earn 21 Kingdom Points before the Adversary across 7 rounds.

Multiplayer (2–4): Most Kingdom Points after 7 rounds wins.

🃏 The Cards

12 parable cards. Each player draws 5 cards at the start. Each card shows: Parable Name & Scripture, Virtue (Faith/Mercy/Wisdom/Generosity), Story Summary, Special Ability, and Kingdom Points (KP).

⚡ Challenges

Each round a Challenge card is revealed. Playing a card whose Virtue matches earns a +2 KP bonus.

🔄 How to Play

  1. A Challenge card is revealed for the round.
  2. The active player selects and plays a card from their hand.
  3. In multiplayer, players take turns. In single player, the AI plays after you.
  4. KP are awarded based on card value + virtue match bonuses + special abilities.
  5. After 7 rounds, the player with the most KP wins.
  6. You may Pass instead of playing — but lose 1 KP.

✨ The Four Virtues

⬡ Faith

Bold trust in God. High KP and powerful effects.

❤ Mercy

Forgiveness and compassion. Can heal lost KP.

◆ Wisdom

Strategic advantages — drawing cards, seeing ahead.

✦ Generosity

Multiplies blessings when shared.

🤖 AI Difficulty

  • Easy: Plays randomly.
  • Medium: Tries to match the challenge virtue.
  • Hard: Always optimizes for maximum KP.
